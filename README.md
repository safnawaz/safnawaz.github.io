# Safiyyah Nawaz, PhD

Professional academic website built with HTML and CSS. View the website at [safnawaz.github.io](https://safnawaz.github.io)

## Features

- **Sleek & Professional Design**: Modern, minimalist academic theme with professional color scheme
- **Responsive Layout**: Fully responsive design that works beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Sticky Navigation**: Easy navigation between sections
- **Customizable Sections**:
  - About
  - Experience & Education
  - Publications
  - Projects
  - Skills & Expertise
  - Contact

## Getting Started

1. Update `index.html` with your information:
   - Replace "Safiyyah Nawaz, PhD" with your name
   - Update your academic title and focus area
   - Add your contact email and social media links
   - Fill in your about section, experience, publications, projects, and skills

2. Customize the color scheme by modifying the CSS variables in the `<style>` section:
   - `--primary-color`: Main header color
   - `--secondary-color`: Secondary accent color
   - `--accent-color`: Highlight color for links and borders

## Sections to Customize

### Header
- Name, title, and subtitle
- Email, LinkedIn, GitHub, ORCID links

### About Section
- Personal introduction and research interests
- Career summary

### Experience Section
- Current and previous positions
- Education and degrees
- Dates and descriptions

### Publications Section
- Journal articles, conference papers, books
- Publication details (authors, journal, DOI)
- Tags for topics

### Projects Section
- Research projects and collaborations
- Dates, roles, and descriptions
- Technologies and methods used

### Skills Section
- Research methods
- Technical skills
- Domain expertise areas

### Contact Section
- Email address
- Office location
- Phone number
