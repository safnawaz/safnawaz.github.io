Musical effects in an online What-Where-When paradigm
Collaborators: Dr. Diana Omigie (supervision), Ellen Murphy (research), Eimear North (research)
I developed an online paradigm based on the What-Where-When paradigm to investigate the effects of music on episodic memory encoding. The study was conducted using PsychoPy and hosted online. In two experiments we explored the effects of emotional music, aesthetic response and liking on encoding of spatio temporal information.
